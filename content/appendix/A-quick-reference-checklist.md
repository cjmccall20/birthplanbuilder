# Quick Reference Decision Checklist

Use this checklist to discuss your preferences with your provider before labor. Check the boxes that align with your goals.

---

## Before Labor

### Birth Setting
- [ ] Hospital
- [ ] Birth center
- [ ] Home birth

### Care Provider
- [ ] OB/GYN
- [ ] Midwife (CNM/CPM)
- [ ] Doula support: Yes / No

### Pain Management Plan
- [ ] Plan for unmedicated birth
- [ ] Open to medication if needed
- [ ] Planning epidural
- [ ] Want nitrous oxide available

---

## During Labor

### Monitoring
- [ ] Prefer intermittent monitoring (if low-risk)
- [ ] Accept continuous monitoring if medically indicated

### IV Access
- [ ] Prefer hep lock over continuous IV
- [ ] Accept IV if medically indicated

### Movement & Comfort
- [ ] Freedom to walk and move
- [ ] Access to shower/tub
- [ ] Ability to eat/drink light snacks
- [ ] Prefer dim lights, quiet environment

### Pushing
- [ ] Prefer spontaneous pushing (follow body's urges)
- [ ] Open to coached pushing if needed with epidural
- [ ] Want to try various positions

---

## Immediately After Birth

### Cord Clamping
- [ ] Delayed cord clamping (until cord stops pulsing)
- [ ] Partner wants to cut cord: Yes / No

### Skin-to-Skin
- [ ] Immediate skin-to-skin for at least 1 hour
- [ ] Delay all non-urgent procedures during golden hour
- [ ] Skin-to-skin during C-section if possible

### First Bath
- [ ] Delay bath at least 24 hours
- [ ] Parents give first bath

---

## Newborn Procedures

### Vitamin K
- [ ] Intramuscular shot (standard)
- [ ] Oral vitamin K (requires multiple doses)

### Eye Ointment
- [ ] Accept (required in most states)
- [ ] Decline if tested negative for STIs (check state law)

### Hepatitis B Vaccine
- [ ] At birth
- [ ] Delay to 2-month visit

### Newborn Screening (Heel Prick)
- [ ] Accept (recommended)

### Hearing Test
- [ ] Accept (recommended)

---

## If GBS Positive

- [ ] Accept IV antibiotics in labor
- [ ] Discussed probiotic options during pregnancy
- [ ] Plan for infant probiotics after birth

---

## Feeding

- [ ] Exclusive breastfeeding
- [ ] Formula feeding
- [ ] Combination feeding
- [ ] No pacifiers until breastfeeding established
- [ ] Pacifiers okay from start

---

## Hospital Stay

- [ ] Rooming in 24/7
- [ ] Use nursery for rest if available
- [ ] Limit visitors
- [ ] Early discharge if healthy

---

## Circumcision (If Applicable)

- [ ] Yes - circumcision
- [ ] No - keeping intact
- [ ] Undecided - want to discuss further
